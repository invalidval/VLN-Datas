UB Building Floor Plans

Authorized users can view online floor plans for all UB buildings. Secure access is required to access the UB floor plans. You may need to run the Cisco Secure Client prior to logging in, depending on your connection.

On this page:

Who Can Access Floor Plans?Using the Floor PlansContact an Expert
Go to Building Floor Plans

Already have authorization to access floor plans? Log in with your UBITName and password.

Who Can Access Floor Plans?

Authorized Users

Access to building floor plans is restricted to authorized groups and personnel for official university purposes. Departments such as University Police, UBIT’s Network & Classroom Service, Residential Facilities and University Facilities can access floor plans without requesting access.

FMPOs

FPMOs can request access on the Managing Facilities for Your Unit page.

Other Requests

All others should submit a formal request through the online FOIL request. Professors who require building information for an individual or class-wide project must submit a FOIL request. Students may not directly request floor plans; requests instead should come from the professor on behalf of the student, and only the specific building floor plans required for the project will be considered. Outside entities also must request access to floor plans and building information through a FOIL request. Those entities contracted to perform work on behalf of UB should work through their Project Manager to request the necessary documentation required for their work.

Student-Related Floor Plans Requests

Information about student housing is available from the offices of Campus Living.  

  • What Does My Room Look Like?
  • Student Living Space — Find Your Home

Using the Floor Plans

UB floor plans are provided in Adobe PDF format. All floor plans support layer control to turn layers on or off, such as furniture and room numbers. AutoCAD embeds the scale of every drawing, requiring a graphic scale obsolete, so you can use Adobe’s built-in measuring tools to measure distances. Lastly, all official UB room numbers automatically generate bookmarks that can be accessed from the bookmarks menu. This will automatically zoom to the selected room without searching around.
